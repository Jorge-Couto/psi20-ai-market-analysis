# PSI20 AI Market Analysis
This project was developed during my internship at BI4ALL as part of the final semester of my Bachelor's degree.

The system combines deep learning-based time-series forecasting with a multi-agent AI architecture to analyze and forecast the short-term evolution of stocks belonging to the PSI20 index. LSTM models are used to generate quantitative price forecasts and estimate predictive uncertainty, while specialized AI agents analyze recent financial news and market information.

The quantitative and qualitative analyses are then combined to produce a final market outlook, ranging from Bearish to Bullish, together with a confidence level and associated risk considerations.
